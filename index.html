Classic Puff-Puff Recipe

Having a nice puff snack can make your day go smoth with no worries.
Craving a nice puff snack? Look no further, Dee's Delight has got you

Ingredients

  • 600g all-purpose flour
  • 10g fast action yeast
  • 150g sugar (use as desired)
  • Â½ tsp salt
  • 550-650g Lukewarm Water
  • 1-3tbsp chilli flakes (substitute with cayenne pepper)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. add flour and salt together (combine salt with flour first)
  2. Add other dry ingredients and combine well
  3. Add lukewarm water to the dry mixture in bits to form a batter
  4. You can add 500g at first then check if it needs more water
  5. Cover the bowl with a cling film or a damp towel and place in a warm place to proof
  6. Once the mixture has risen and doubled in size, you then move on to frying
