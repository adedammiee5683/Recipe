Classic Puff-Puff Recipe

Are you craving a mouth-watering sweet treat?
Our Puff-Puff Balls are incredibly easy to make
and will quickly become a favourite part of your love after one bite.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups plain flour
  • 1 cup caster sugar/granulated sugar
  • 4 tsp. instant yeast
  • sprinkle of Nutmeg
  • 2 cups of Vegetable Oil
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 2 cups of warm water

METHOD

  1. sift flour
  2. mix it
  3. allow it
  4. fry it
